About Us

We want to bring language learning people together

The Origin of the Idea

The idea for this platform came up during the Hack for Refugees-Event in Gothenburg-Sweden

The Idea

The idea of this platform is to bring volunteers who want to help refugees and refugees who want to learn the local language together.

Events can be created by volunteers as well as big organisations and refugees can find these events and reserve a seat.

How can I help?

If you know a language the refugees are native in and want to teach them the countries native language, just sign up, create an event and teach the refugees.

If you are a big organisation that has a great idea for an event but lacks the volunteers for it - we are here to help out. As an organisation you can find volunteers in your area and ask them if they want to help with your event.
